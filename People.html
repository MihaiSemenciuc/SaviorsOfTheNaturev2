Meet Our Team

- We are a group of likeminded individuals who want to make the planet a better place -

Michael Ford was born in a small town in Minnesota and studied there until he got to college. After completing his studies, he managed to create his own volunteer organization under the name of Saviors Of Nature, which today brings together volunteers from around the globe. He is the founder of the organization, he is a very open-minded man who supports his volunteers with everything he can.

Michael Ford

Founder

Jasmin Becker is the head of fundraising. Jasmin graduated from the Faculty of Economics in Los Angeles and chose to join the organization in 2018 when she managed to collaborate with Michael in a project to save wild animals in Africa, and later became a member of the organization. She is a very important pawn in the organization.

Jasmin Becker

Fund Raiser

In 2019 Michael meets Kara, she is an active volunteer in afforestation campaigns, from the beginning Kara showed a huge interest in saving the planet, and will later be invited to be part of the organization, she being the pawn that it transmits all the information to the teams of volunteers from all over the planet, managing to recruit new people every year.

Kara Barrons

Volunteer Team Leader

Lucia Boven is the main pawn in the natural field, she deals with all the projects in this framework, coordinating them and transmitting all the information to Michael, about the number of volunteers needed or any problem related to the activities in this field. She contacts the environment ministries of each country where the organization manages to impose projects and sets up their start-up plan as soon as possible. Lucia was born in Las Vegas and studied at the Faculty of Biotechnology in New York, being passionate about nature and natural technologies.

Lucia Boven

He claims that with the help of volunteers, the planet can be saved.
