Our Work

- Take some time to see our work -
Trees Planting

Over time, our organization has organized countless volunteer activities for planting trees in endangered areas. In the last ten years our organization has managed to plant over one million trees, and our volunteers have managed to take part in afforestations around the world.

Plant >
Saving The Oceans

In the last 5 years, our organization has managed to collect more than ten tons of marine plastic and clean several beaches in both Europe and the USA. We try to realise as many projects in this area as possible because the amount of plastic in the oceans is worrying.

Animals Saving

Although this area is quite difficult to reach out, our organization has managed to build a shelter for stray animals and get involved in the protection of endangered animal species.
