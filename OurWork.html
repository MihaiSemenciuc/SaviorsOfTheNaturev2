Our Work

- Take some time to appreciate our work -

Trees Planting

Our organization started nature saving projects through afforestation in 2017, just one year after the founding of this organization. We can be proud that we managed to afforest over 2000 cubic meters of land with the help of volunteers from all over the world. One of the most challenging afforestations was in Romania where the theft of wood is massive and the country is struggling with problems in this area. Even if it was harder, we managed with the Romanian volunteers to plant over 2000 seedlings and to create a beautiful friendship with the people there. The volunteers were also dedicated and very hardworking people.We plan to expand in the area of ​​Ukraine but also in Poland. We are waiting for volunteers from all over the world, you just have to want to contribute to a better future of the entire planet. We fight for a better life and cleaner air.Our next afforestation project targets the rebirth of an entire forest in a deforested area of ​​Austria.We shouted for the volunteers' help and we are waiting them with our arms opened.

Oceans Saving

In recent years, the organization Saviors Of Nature has managed to focus on the aquatic field. Over time, the organization has managed to create projects that cover saving beaches in both the United States and Europe. In the last two ocean plastic collection projects, our volunteer teams have managed to collect over two tons of marine plastic. It is inhuman what is happening on this planet today. The image of marine animals killed by plastic is terrifying. Our organization tries to create as many projects in this field as possible, because the situation is worrying. The next ocean rescue project will take place this summer, for which registration has already opened as a volunteer. We are waiting for you in large numbers because only in this way can we clean the planet.The world must understand that the planet is not an infinite source of resources and that it must be protected at all costs until it is too late.

Animals Saving

Our organization started the activities of rescuing homeless animals since 2017 when Kassandra Nade our wildlife team leader came up with the proposal to build an animal shelter, which was done less than two months after the proposal. In recent years, we have managed to get involved with her in consolidating several shelters but also in rescuing wild or endangered animals. In recent years, fires in Australia have posed a tragic and devastating threat to animals, so our organization has been involved in rescuing animals in Australia from those difficult times We hope that in the coming years we will join other more experienced organizations in this field such as WWF. Our next project in this area will be the rescue of the endangered turtle species from South Africa, where we want to build a shelter for injured wild animals, all our funds going to build a hospital for wild animals.
